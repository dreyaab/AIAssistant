Your Burning Questions Answered: A Q&A Session on what an AI-Assisted Vanity is

Get ready for an enlightening Q&A session where we tackle all your inquiries!

What is an AI Assisted Vanity?

It’s a vanity that is not just portable, but it can also help you with what kind of look you’re going for. The AI doesn’t just helps you with the looks,
it also has filters which can help you find out you’re face shape, etc. Besides those two, we also included quizzes which can help you what kind of looks look good on you.

What makes it unique?

It’s unlike any other vanity. Other vanities are big and in need of a big space. This vanity is portable, which means you can bring it everywhere and is suitable
for small spaces. Not just that, but it’s also AI assisted.

How does the AI work??

The AI doesn’t just recommend you looks, it will also help you with your face shape, what type of products are good for you, tips and tricks, etc!
By using the filters and answering the quizzes, you will be able to know the answers for the following:
Face Shape.
Cool, warm, neutral toned.
Low or High Visual Weight

What was the inspiration behind this vanity?

Thank you, to Ayesha Juera for suggesting the AI, but the main inspiration for this vanity is Cher’s closet from the movie Clueless. The closet is designed to take clothes inside your
closet and make recommendations based on the weather and machine-learned style preferences.
