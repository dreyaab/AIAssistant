Discover Your Perfect Makeup Look: A Comprehensive Guide to Finding Your Signature Style

Expert guidance and tutorials to uncover your signature look, whether it's natural elegance or bold glam, empowering you with confidence and creativity.

1. Natural Makeup.

This can sometimes be described as “No-Makeup Makeup” or “Second Skin.” This look defines your features without major changes,
sculpting, or colors. Simply put… it's minimal makeup! Think your everyday makeup routine... elevated!

2. Clean Girl Look.

The clean girl make up enhances looks and gives your skin permission to let its natural beauty shine. Its a good make up look if you want a
simple make up since its less use of foundation and its not pore clogging and heavy make up look.

3. Dark Feminine Look.

This make up look is more intense and gives off a DGAF vibes, it looks fierce and unique. It mostly looks like a high arched brows, dark
smokey coloured eye shadow, partnered up with red lipstick or mostly nude lip colors

4. Douyin Makeup.

Douyin make up is one of the chinese popular make up aesthetics, it features glowy, translucent skin, glittery eyelids and a
mauve colored blush. It gained popularity in Tiktok western countries.

5. Igari Makeup.

Igari make up is a simple make up for most japanese women, also known as drunk blush makeup, is an easy-to-achieve makeup style by
applying blush (from the left cheek across the nose bridge to the right cheek). It's named Igari after the renowned Japanese makeup artist, Igari Shinobu.

6. Latina Makeup.

Have you heard about 'Copy and paste Latina' looks? It got popular in tiktok, Latina make up is mostly includes straight black hair,
dark eyebrows, full lashes, and brown lip-liner. usually have jet black straight hair and wear a full face of makeup with big lashes.”
The trend originates from TikTok and was inspired by the appearance of Becky G

7. Full Glam Makeup.

A full glam make up is mostly a heavy makeup, with multiple layers, contouring, baking. Thick strip lashes and heavy cut creases.
It takes about 60 minutes to finish. This makeup look is often associated with special occasions, events, or performances where a more intense and
eye-catching appearance is desired.

8. ABG Look.

From makeup and style tutorials to skits, the “Asian Baby Girl,” or ABG trend has been on the rise in recent years. The ABG look, according to the internet , is a fashion and
lifestyle trend embodied by Asian American women who often sport long, dyed hair, eyelash extensions, manicured nails, tattoos, and tanned skin.

9. Filipino Makeup.

Filipino Makeup AKA Liptint and Pulbo look is a very popular look in the Philippines. Out of all the looks that was mentioned, this is probably the most simple and easy
look out of all of them. It only includes Liptint and Powder. For the base, you only have to use powder. Baby powder is commonly used.
For the blush, lips, and eyeshadow, you only have to use a liptint.



10. Grunge Glam or 90s Look.

Grunge Glam. Grunge glam, a quintessential 90s makeup look, perfectly captured the era's rebellious spirit. With smudged eyeliner,
moody eyeshadows, and matte lips, it celebrated the art of imperfection.

11. 80s Look

A typical make up look in the 80's are mostly Blue eyeshadow, also known for big hair, and neon lips, more likely a bright make up looks and
it was very popular in the 80's. This doesn't really give off a light makeup loo

12. 60s Look.

The '60s makeup is most famous for its dramatic eye looks with bold eyeliners and long, luscious lashes. Due to the bold eye makeup,
muted shades were used for lipsticks, such as pale pink, light orange, and nude. The most popular eyeshadow shades in the '60s were black, gray, white, and blue.
